# Community Meetings

Information about CAC Ontology community meetings and events.

## Upcoming Meetings

Community meetings are scheduled regularly to discuss:

- Project updates and roadmap
- Technical discussions
- Use case sharing
- Planning and coordination

### Meeting Schedule

Meeting schedules will be announced through:

- GitHub Discussions
- Project VIC International channels
- Community mailing lists

## Meeting Types

### Technical Meetings

Focus on:
- Ontology development
- Module design and implementation
- Validation and testing
- Integration with CASE/UCO

### User Meetings

Focus on:
- Use case sharing
- Best practices
- Tool demonstrations
- Community support

### Planning Meetings

Focus on:
- Roadmap planning
- Feature prioritization
- Community organization
- Strategic direction

## Past Meetings

Meeting notes and recordings (when available) will be shared through:

- GitHub Discussions
- Project documentation
- Community channels

## How to Participate

### Join a Meeting

1. Check for meeting announcements in:
   - GitHub Discussions
   - Community channels
   - Project VIC International communications

2. Register or RSVP (if required)

3. Attend and participate

### Propose a Meeting Topic

To propose a meeting topic:

1. Open a discussion in [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Project-VIC-International/CAC-Ontology/discussions)
2. Describe the topic and its relevance
3. Suggest a format (presentation, discussion, workshop)

## Meeting Guidelines

- **Respectful Communication** - Maintain professional and respectful discourse
- **Inclusive Participation** - Welcome contributions from all community members
- **Documentation** - Meeting notes will be shared when available
- **Time Management** - Meetings will be structured to respect participants' time
