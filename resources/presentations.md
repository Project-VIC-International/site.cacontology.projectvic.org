# Presentations

Conference presentations, talks, and webinars about CAC Ontology.

## Upcoming Presentations

Information about upcoming presentations will be posted here.

## Past Presentations

Presentations and talks about CAC Ontology will be listed here as they become available.

## Presentation Topics

Presentations may cover:

- **Ontology Overview** - Introduction to CAC Ontology and its capabilities
- **Use Cases** - Real-world applications and case studies
- **Technical Details** - Architecture, design, and implementation
- **Integration** - Working with CASE and UCO
- **Validation** - SHACL validation and data quality
- **Community** - Community development and contributions

## Contributing Presentations

If you have given a presentation about CAC Ontology:

1. Contact the project maintainers through [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Project-VIC-International/CAC-Ontology/issues) or [Contact page](/contact)
2. Provide:
   - Presentation title
   - Presenter(s)
   - Event/conference name
   - Date
   - Link to slides or recording (if available)
   - Brief description

3. Presentations will be reviewed and added to this page
