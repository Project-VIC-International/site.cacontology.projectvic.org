## Contributing Publications

If you have published research or articles about CAC Ontology:

1. Contact the project maintainers through [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Project-VIC-International/CAC-Ontology/issues) or [Contact page](/contact)
2. Provide:
   - Publication title
   - Authors
   - Publication venue/conference/journal
   - Link to publication (if available)
   - Abstract or summary

3. Publications will be reviewed and added to this page

## Citation

When citing CAC Ontology in publications, please use:

```
CAC Ontology Family. Project VIC International. 
https://cacontology.projectvic.org
```

Or cite specific modules as appropriate.
